# Spanish affixes in Purepecha/Tarascan


Information and examples are from [Chamoreau ](sources.bib?label=Chamoreau+#cldf:chamoreau2012a)([2012](sources.bib?label=2012#cldf:chamoreau2012a):81–82).


1 diminutive

- _-itu_ ‘diminutive’, e.g. _watsi-itu-etʃa-ni_ (boy-diminutive-pl-object) ‘(to) the boys’, _xantiakhu-itu_ ‘completely alone’ (from _xantiakhu_ ‘alone’), _sapilati-itu-e-p-ka_ (small-diminutive-predication-aorist.past-subject) ‘(you) were very young’
