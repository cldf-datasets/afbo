# Latvian affixes in German


This case refers to German dialects spoken in Latvia. Information and examples are from [Polanska ](sources.bib?label=Polanska+#cldf:polanska2002a)([2002](sources.bib?label=2002#cldf:polanska2002a):161–167).


1 nominal derivational suffix

- _-neck_ ‘occupation noun, agent noun’, e.g. _kammerneck_ ‘peasant (lit. inhabitant of a small chamber)’, _reiseneck_ ‘traveller’, _vlosseneck_ ‘rafter’
