# English affixes in Hawaiian


Information and examples are from [Parker Jones ](sources.bib?label=Parker+Jones+#cldf:parker2009a)([2009](sources.bib?label=2009#cldf:parker2009a):784).


1 derivational suffix

- _-kona_ ‘sports event’ (from English _marathon_), e.g. _hele-kona_ ‘walk-athon’ (from _hele_ ‘go’)


A number of other English affixes in Hawaiian are not attested with native stems, i.e. _heko-kalame_ ‘hecto-gram’, _keni-kalame_ ‘centi-gram’, _polai-posapahate_ ‘poly-phosphate’.
