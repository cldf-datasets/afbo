# Persian affixes in Lezgian


Information and examples are from [Haspelmath ](sources.bib?label=Haspelmath+#cldf:haspelmath1993a)([1993](sources.bib?label=1993#cldf:haspelmath1993a):107) and from Don Stilo (personal communication 2013).


3 nominalizers (out of a total of 8 nominalizers in Lezgian)

- _-qʰan_ ‘agent nouns from nouns’, e.g. _xpe-qʰan_ ‘sheep herder’
- _-gan_ ‘nouns denoting containers from nouns’, e.g. _t’urar-gan_ ‘box for spoons’
- _-ban_ ‘nouns denoting people associated with the thing denoted by the base word’, e.g. _wak’ar-ban_ ‘swineherd’
