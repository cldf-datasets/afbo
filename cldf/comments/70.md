# Estonian affixes in Ingrian Finnish


Information and examples are from [Riionheimo ](sources.bib?label=Riionheimo+#cldf:riionheimo2002a)([2002](sources.bib?label=2002#cldf:riionheimo2002a); [2010](sources.bib?label=2010#cldf:riionheimo2010a)). Note that Finnish and Estonian are closely related.


2 tense (and person) markers

- _-p_ ‘third person present’, e.g. _maksa-a-p_ ‘it costs’, _istu-u-p_ ‘he sits’, _tullo-o-p_ ‘he comes’
- _-si_ ‘past’, e.g. _ve-i-si-mme_ ‘we took’_, osta-si-it_ ‘they bought it’, _jouta-si-mme_ ‘we were in time’
