# Spanish affixes in Yucatec Maya


Information and examples are from [Chamoreau ](sources.bib?label=Chamoreau+#cldf:chamoreau2012a)([2012](sources.bib?label=2012#cldf:chamoreau2012a):84).


1 diminutive

- _-ito ~ -itu_ ‘diminutive’, e.g. _polok-ito_ ‘sort of fat (of a male)’, _bek’ech-ita_ ‘sort of thin (of a female)’, _sak-ito_ ‘sort of white (of a man)’, _xlo’obayan-ita_ ‘youthful (of a female)’
