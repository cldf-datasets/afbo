# Portuguese affixes in Tetun Dili


Information and examples are from [Hajek ](sources.bib?label=Hajek+#cldf:hajek2006a)([2006](sources.bib?label=2006#cldf:hajek2006a):172).


1 nominalizer

- _-dor_ ‘agent nominalizer’, someone who habitually does something (often pejorative), i.e. more restricted than the general agentive noun derivation in Portuguese, e.g. _hemudór_ ‘drinker’ (from _hemu_ ‘drink’) “the only Portuguese affix that can be applied to a native root” according to [Hajek ](sources.bib?label=Hajek+#cldf:hajek2006a)([2006](sources.bib?label=2006#cldf:hajek2006a):172).
