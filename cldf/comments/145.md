# Chinese affixes in Dagur


Information and examples are from [Tsumagari ](sources.bib?label=Tsumagari+#cldf:tsumagari2003a)([2003](sources.bib?label=2003#cldf:tsumagari2003a):136).


1 plural suffix (out of 4 Dagur plural suffixes)

- -_cieng_ ‘plural’, e.g. _gambul-cieng_ ‘executives’ (in Chinese, _-cieng_ denotes place of origin or habitation, e.g. _batgen-cieng_ ‘Butha people’, _degidee-cieng_ ‘upper-river-dwellers’)
