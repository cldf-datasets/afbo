# intermediate Proto-Carib affixes in Proto Wayampi-Emerillon-Zo’é


Information and examples are from [Rose ](sources.bib?label=Rose+#cldf:rose2012a)([2012](sources.bib?label=2012#cldf:rose2012a)). Detailed reconstruction allows determining that the plural marker was borrowed from an intermediate Proto-Carib language into Proto Wayampi-Emerillon-Zo’é. The marker is a suffix in two daughter languages (Wayampi and Emerillon) and a free form in Zo’é.


1 plural suffix

- _-kom_ (Emerillon), _-kõ_ (Wayampi) ‘plural’, e.g. _wãĩwĩ-kom_ (women-pl) ‘women’ (Emerillon), _e-mɛmɨ-kũ_ (1sg-child-pl) ‘my children’ (Wayampi)
