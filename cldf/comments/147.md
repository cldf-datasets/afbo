# Evenki affixes in Dagur


Information and examples are from [Tsumagari ](sources.bib?label=Tsumagari+#cldf:tsumagari2003a)([2003](sources.bib?label=2003#cldf:tsumagari2003a):136).


3 plural suffixes (out of a total of 4 Dagur plural markers)

- _-sul_ ‘plural’ (used with any noun), e.g. _akaa-sul_ ‘elder brothers’, _mori-sul_ ‘horses’, _mood-sul_ ‘trees’
- -_nur_ ‘plural’ (for human nouns), e.g. _guc/i-nur_ ‘friends’, _deu-nur_ ‘younger brothers’
- -_r_ ‘plural’ (for human nouns), e.g. _ugi-r_ ‘girls’, _uciike-r_ ‘infants’, _kek/u-r_ ‘children’
