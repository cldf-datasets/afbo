# Ilokano affixes in Guina-ang Bontok


Information and examples are from Lawrence Reid (personal communication, January 2012).


3 non-interrelated forms

- _=to/=nto_ ‘future’, encliticized to predicate, used in addition to inherited, preverbal future auxiliary. Only occurs in speech of younger generation (as of early 21st century), e.g. _mapan=ka=nto_ ‘you will go’
- _mang-/nang-_ ‘completed aspect, intransitive’, replaced native _ma_{_n,n,ng_}- +deletion of initial consonant of stem, e.g. _nang-sulbar_ ‘(they) solved’
- _taga-_ ‘a person from’ derivation, replaced native _i-_; could be from Ilokano or Tagalog, e.g. _tagabaryo_ ‘someone from the neighborhood’
